FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. What is Byte Bank?

Byte Bank is a digital-first bank offering financial services, investments, and wealth management solutions.

2. How do I open an account?

You can open an account online by visiting our website, clicking "Sign Up," and following the steps.

3. Is my money safe with Byte Bank?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and multi-factor authentication to ensure your funds and data are secure.

4. What investment options does Byte Bank offer?

We provide automated investing, wealth management, and personalized advisory services.

5. Is there a minimum investment amount?

Yes, our automated investing service starts at $1,000, while advisory services start at $100,000.

6. Can I access my account on mobile?

Yes, the Byte Bank mobile app allows you to manage your finances anywhere, anytime.

7. Does Byte Bank charge fees?

For all checking accounts, excluding student accounts, a monthly maintenance fee of $2.50 applies.
For each transaction, non-members are charged $5, while bank members are charged $1.50.
Payments made through the website or mobile application are exempt from any transaction fees.

8. Can I make international money transfers?

Yes, we support international money transfers with competitive exchange rates.

9. What benefits does a Byte Bank Wealth Account offer?

It provides professional portfolio management, financial advisory, and exclusive investment opportunities.

10. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via mail, phone support, or through our mobile app.

11. Can I open a joint account?

Yes, joint accounts are available for couples and business partners.

12. Does Byte Bank offer loans?

Yes, we provide personal and business loans with competitive interest rates.

13. Can I set up automatic bill payments?

Yes, you can schedule recurring bill payments through our online banking platform.

14. How do I reset my password?
